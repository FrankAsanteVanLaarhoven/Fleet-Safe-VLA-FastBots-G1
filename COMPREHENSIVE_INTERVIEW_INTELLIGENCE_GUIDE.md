## 🚀 **Quick Start Guide**

### **Step 1: Install Dependencies**
```bash
pip install SpeechRecognition pyaudio
```

### **Step 2: Launch System**
```bash
python comprehensive_interview_intelligence.py
```

### **Step 3: Conduct Interview**
1. **Select Domain** from dropdown menu
2. **Click "Start Interview"** when interview begins
3. **System records** transcript and analyzes responses
4. **Click "End Interview"** when complete
5. **Click "Analyze Interview"** for detailed analysis
6. **Click "Generate Report"** for downloadable report

## 🎓 **PhD-Level Topic Analysis**

### **Why Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Understanding motivations, reasons, and underlying causes
- **Questions**: Why did you choose this approach? Why is this solution optimal?
- **Framework**: Motivational factors, causal relationships, strategic reasoning

### **Who Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Identifying stakeholders, team dynamics, and responsibilities
- **Questions**: Who are the key stakeholders? Who would be responsible?
- **Framework**: Stakeholder mapping, role identification, team composition

### **What Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Defining objectives, requirements, and deliverables
- **Questions**: What are the specific requirements? What is the expected outcome?
- **Framework**: Requirement analysis, scope definition, deliverable specification

### **Where Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Understanding context, location, and environment
- **Questions**: Where will this be implemented? Where are the bottlenecks?
- **Framework**: Context analysis, location mapping, environment assessment

### **When Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Timeline planning, sequencing, and temporal factors
- **Questions**: When should this be completed? When are the critical milestones?
- **Framework**: Timeline planning, milestone identification, critical path analysis

### **How Analysis**
- **Purpose**: Implementation strategy, methodology, and execution
- **Questions**: How will this be implemented? How do we measure progress?
- **Framework**: Implementation strategy, methodology selection, execution plan

## 📊 **Analysis Metrics**

### **Sentiment Analysis**
- **Positive Responses**: Confident, enthusiastic, knowledgeable
- **Negative Responses**: Uncertain, hesitant, confused
- **Neutral Responses**: Balanced, factual, measured
- **Sentiment Trends**: Changes throughout interview

### **Confidence Scoring**
- **High Confidence (70-100%)**: Definite, certain, clear responses
- **Medium Confidence (30-69%)**: Probable, likely, think responses
- **Low Confidence (0-29%)**: Unsure, uncertain, doubtful responses
- **Confidence Trends**: Improvement or decline during interview

### **Topic Coverage**
- **Keyword Detection**: Domain-specific terms and concepts
- **Topic Frequency**: How often topics are discussed
- **Topic Depth**: Level of detail in responses
- **Topic Breadth**: Range of subjects covered

### **Overall Rating Calculation**
- **Sentiment Score**: Ratio of positive responses
- **Confidence Score**: Average confidence across responses
- **Topic Coverage Score**: Number of topics discussed
- **Combined Rating**: Weighted average of all factors
